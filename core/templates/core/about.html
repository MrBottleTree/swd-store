About SWD Store

What is SWD Store?

SWD Store is a student-run marketplace for BITS Pilani campuses — Goa, Hyderabad, Pilani, and Dubai. It lets students buy and sell second-hand items easily within their campus community, replacing the older pawnshop platform with a cleaner, faster experience.

Built by

SWD Store was designed and built by Vishrut, a student at BITS Pilani Goa Campus. The goal was to create a permanent, well-maintained platform that the student community can rely on.

How it works

  • Sign in with your BITS Google account
  • Browse listings filtered to your campus by default
  • List items for sale with photos and a price
  • Connect with buyers directly over WhatsApp
